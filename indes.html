Have Questions? We Have Answers.

How can I place an order on StyleLoom?

Browse our website, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout following the on-screen instructions.

Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?

Once an order is confirmed, modifications or cancellations may not be possible. Please review carefully before purchasing.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, net banking, and selected digital wallets.

How do I initiate a return?

Visit our Returns page and follow the instructions to start the return process.

How can I track my order?

After dispatch, you’ll receive a tracking number via email to monitor your order.

At this time, we don't offer direct product exchanges. If you'd like a different item, please initiate a return and place a new order.
